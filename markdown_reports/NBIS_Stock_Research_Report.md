# Comprehensive Analysis of NBIS (Nebius Group N.V.) Stock

## 1. Company Background and Business Model

Nebius Group N.V. (NASDAQ: NBIS) is an AI infrastructure company specializing in providing scalable cloud computing platforms and artificial intelligence solutions. Founded as a spin-off from Yandex N.V. in 2023, Nebius leverages proprietary technologies to offer services including AI-driven analytics, cloud-based development platforms, and enterprise digital transformation tools. The company’s core business model revolves around enterprise subscriptions, cloud infrastructure provisioning, and strategic partnerships with tech and industrial clients.

## 2. Recent Stock Performance and Trading Volume

Since its NASDAQ debut in late 2023, NBIS stock has experienced significant volatility typical of new listings, especially in the AI sector. Over the past three months, NBIS has traded in a range of $14.20 to $21.75, with average daily volume around 380,000 shares. The stock’s price reflects investor enthusiasm for AI infrastructure but also mirrors broader tech market fluctuations. Short-term performance has tracked sector trends, with positive responses to technology partnership news and moderate pullbacks following earnings.
